ü¶¥ Fun Dog Facts

Did you know? Dogs have an incredible sense of smell, up to 100,000 times better than humans!

Dogs belong to the biological family Canidae, which includes wolves, foxes, and other dog-like mammals.

Some dogs, like Border Collies, can understand up to 1,000 words!

Dogs dream just like humans! If you see a dog twitching in its sleep, it might be dreaming of running or playing.

üêï Dog Species and Wild Relatives

While domesticated dogs (Canis lupus familiaris) are our loyal pets, their wild relatives include:

  • Gray Wolf (Canis lupus) - The ancestor of all modern dogs.
  • Red Fox (Vulpes vulpes) - A distant cousin known for its cunning nature.
  • African Wild Dog (Lycaon pictus) - A highly social and skilled hunter.
  • Coyote (Canis latrans) - Found mainly in North America, known for its adaptability.
  • Dhole (Cuon alpinus) - A lesser-known wild dog species from Asia.
